The City of London itself is also known as the Square Mile because it's 1.12 square miles in area

The City of London is the ancient core of London and its financial center. The Romans founded it as Londinium, and it has kept its medieval boundaries. The City of London Corporation is the local authority for the City of London, and is responsible for policing and other duties. The name London has changed over time, and has included other names such as Lundin, Londoun, Lunden, and Londen. The Romans may have adapted the name from pre-Celtic words meaning "wide, flowing river".
